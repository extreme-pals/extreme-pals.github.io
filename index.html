About

InfPals

InfPals is a Peer Learning and Support Program, aiming to bring together Year 1 students and facilitate discussions between them. Long story short, we are the best group of people around.

ExtremePals

Want to enter a hackathon but feeling daunted by its unfamiliarity? Well, ExtremePals is for you!

ExtremePals is a NEVER-BEFORE-SEEN game-developing event hosted by InfPals, where you can join a team and create your very own masterpiece. Level-up your Java skills with some hands-on experience, and don't worry if you're unfamiliar with Java! With a dedicated team of InfPals leaders around, you'll never be short of guidance (also guides are provided). You can see this as an introduction to your hackathon journey.
